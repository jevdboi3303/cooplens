# CoopLens Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 5, 2026_

## What CoopLens collects

CoopLens collects only what is necessary to score co-op job postings:

- **Email address** — used to create your account via Supabase authentication
- **Resume text** — uploaded as a PDF, parsed to extract skills, and stored securely to power CV matching scores
- **Job posting content** — text from postings you open on learninginmotion.uvic.ca is sent to our backend for scoring and is not stored permanently
- **Score history** — posting IDs, titles, companies, and scores are stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage) and optionally synced to our database

## What CoopLens does NOT collect

- We do not collect your UVic NetLink credentials
- We do not read or store any pages outside of learninginmotion.uvic.ca
- We do not sell, share, or monetise your data in any way
- We do not use your data for advertising

## Third-party services

- **Supabase** — stores your account and resume data. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Groq** — job posting descriptions are sent to Groq's API (Llama 3.3 70B) to generate scores. Groq does not train on API data. [Groq Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/)
- **Railway** — hosts the backend API. [Railway Privacy Policy](https://railway.app/legal/privacy)

## Data retention

- Your resume and account data are retained until you delete your account
- Score history stored in your browser can be cleared by uninstalling the extension
- You can request deletion of all server-side data by emailing harsadh32@gmail.com
